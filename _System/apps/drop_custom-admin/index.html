Welcome to your Website Admin Portal

Here are a few simple instructions on how to update your site. Any complex changes should be made by your web designer

Adding News Items to the News Page.

1. To add a news item click on the Web Apps icon in the left panel.

2. Click on "news"

3. Click on the "Add Item" button then follow the instructions on that page

Changing Text on your site

To change any existing text on your website click on the EDIT at the very top of this page (next to your business name) This will take you to the webiste where you can navigate to the page you want to edit. Click on the text you want to edit. A new window will open, find the text you want to change and change it! Click "update" & if you are happy with your change click "publish" at the bottom of the page or if you are not happy with it click "discard changes" and try again. Don't worry that the page looks different in "Edit Mode" it will look normal again once you exit the edit mode

Some useful tips

Click on the "REPORTS" tab on the left. There are lots of interesting reports on who is visiting your website, where they come from and what search terms they are using.

Finally

If you have any questions just contact your webdesigner he will be able to help you if you are confused or if you get stuck.
